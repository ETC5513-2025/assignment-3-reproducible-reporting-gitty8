STUDY LIMITATIONS

  • Scholarships and grants were not considered, yet these can significantly lower actual costs.

  • Exchange rates fluctuate over time, which could impact affordability.

  • Language, cultural preferences, and long-term goals (e.g., migration, job prospects) were also not included, though they‚Äôre critical for many students.
